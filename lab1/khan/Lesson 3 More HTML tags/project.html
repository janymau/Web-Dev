Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 H
  • Serves: 4 P
Ingredients Quantity
beef 500 gr
salt 30 gr
Olive oil 100 gr
pepper 30 gr
garlic 2 piece

Step 1: Take your beef and add salt with pepper.

Step 2: Prepare your pan, add oil and turn on the fire.

Step 3: Put beef on a pan, wait 5 minutes and reverse the side, add garlics.
